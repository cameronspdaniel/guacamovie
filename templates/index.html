Welcome to GuacaMovie!

GuacaMovie is an interactive website that visualizes correlations between various movie success measurements based on prior movie data. Follow along and click through the side to gain insights on the movie industry!




Adjusted Box Office Gross

This page allows you to view movies' adjusted gross and filter your selections based on year, studio, overall rank, or movie title.

Check it out!

IMDB Scores By Country

This page allows you to explore the average IMDB score based on the origin country of the production company. Our data goes back to 1986 and includes data from 57 different countries!
